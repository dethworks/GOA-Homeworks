Welcome to the World of Batman

Who is Batman?

Batman, also known as the Dark Knight, is a superhero who defends Gotham City from crime and corruption. His real identity is Bruce Wayne, a billionaire and philanthropist.

  • Bane

Batman's Gadgets

From the iconic Batarang to the Batmobile, Batman uses a variety of high-tech gadgets to fight crime and navigate the shadows of Gotham City.
